Branding & Design Asthetic

The first step in this project was to create a style guide that represented the websites branding and design asethetic. To begin I narrowed down colours, fonts and some potential layout ideas. For example, I took inspiration for traditional Indian bridal colours for the main colour scheme.

Before creating the elements in HTML/CSS, I designed them in Figma then translated them into code.
